About Fly By Jing

Are you bored of bland, lackluster meals? Craving something that will awaken your taste buds? Prepare to meet your new obsession: Fly By Jing, the first premium Sichuan chili sauce, perfected over two years of cooking experimentation, and lovingly crafted in the culinary capital of Chengdu, China.

What exactly is Chinese Chili Sauce called?

While it answers to many names like “Chili Oil” or “Chili Garlic Sauce,” we like to call it “Sichuan Chili Crisp” or “Zhong Sauce”. And it possesses an addictive quality that will leave you craving more.

So, what is in Chinese Chili Sauce?

It’s a blend of chili peppers, minced garlic, chili flakes, aromatic spices, and chili oil to create a mesmerizing flavor profile that satisfies your cravings and leaves you utterly powerless to its irresistible deliciousness.

What sets our sauces apart?

It's simple: we believe in the power of nature and the art of true craftsmanship. We start by sourcing the finest all-natural ingredients sourced straight from Jing's hometown of Chengdu! Each bottle is a taste explosion, carefully crafted in small batches to ensure maximum freshness.

Our sauces are made without any GMOs, MSG, or artificial preservatives. We don't need those crutches to create mind-blowing flavors. Instead, we let the magic of our carefully selected ingredients shine through, elevating your meals to a whole new level.

It's all thanks to a perfect balance of savory, spicy, and umami flavors. And our sauces are not just meant for traditional dishes. Think outside the bowl and let your imagination run wild. Try Fly By Jing's homemade chili sauce on pizza, eggs, or even ice cream. Yes, you read that right—ice cream! We are all about pushing boundaries and making your taste buds go wild.

Where to buy Chinese chili sauce from?

Take a spin through our chili hot sauce options and homemade chili garlic sauce recipe. From the original Sichuan Chili Crisp to Xtra Spicy Chili Crisp and the mouthwatering Zhong Sauce inspired by traditional Chinese street fare, we’ve got something for everyone.

Let's change the game when it comes to the perception of Chinese cuisine and celebrate diverse cultures through the power of flavor. Fly By Jing is here to make your taste buds dance with joy and bring the irresistible allure of Sichuan straight to your table. Get ready to rock your taste buds and savor every tongue-tingling bite!
